Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information on MacWin.

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, and contact information
  • Profile information and preferences
  • Content you post, share, or upload
  • Messages and communications with other users
  • Device and usage information

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Personalize your experience
  • Communicate with you about updates and changes
  • Ensure platform security and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

We process your information based on your consent, our legitimate interests, and legal requirements.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in specific circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements or court orders

  • With service providers who assist in platform operations

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of certain data uses and communications
  • Export your data in a portable format

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. This includes encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
